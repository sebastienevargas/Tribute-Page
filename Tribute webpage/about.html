Early Life
October 28, 1987:
Born in Long Beach, California.
1987-1992:
Moved to New Orleans, Louisiana, with his family when he was five years old.
Career Beginnings
2005:
Hurricane Katrina destroyed his home and recording studio in New Orleans, prompting him to transfer to the University of Louisiana at Lafayette.
2006:
Moved to Los Angeles to pursue a music career.
2009:
Met members of the hip hop collective Odd Future, including Tyler, the Creator.
2009:
Signed with Def Jam Recordings.
Early Releases
February 16, 2011:
Released his debut mixtape, "Nostalgia, Ultra", which included the songs "Novacane" and "Swim Good".
May 2011:
Released the single "Novacane" on iTunes.
Major Albums
July 10, 2012:
Released his debut studio album, "Channel Orange", which included the songs "Thinkin Bout You", "Pyramids", "Sweet Life", "Lost", and "Super Rich Kids".
August 19, 2016:
Released the visual album "Endless", which included the song "Sideways".
August 20, 2016:
Released his second studio album, "Blonde", which included the songs "Nikes", "Ivy", "Pink + White", "Solo", "Skyline To", "Self Control", "White Ferrari", "Seigfried", "Godspeed", and "Futura Free".
