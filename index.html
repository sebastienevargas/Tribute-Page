He is widely recognized as a pioneer of the alternative R&B genre

White Ferrari" is a standout track from Frank Ocean's album Blonde. The song is a deeply introspective and poetic reflection on love, time, and the fleeting nature of life . The imagery of a white Ferrari serves as a metaphor for a journey, both literal and figurative, representing the ride of a relationship .
